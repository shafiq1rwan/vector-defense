HOW TO PLAY
NEON GRID TOWER DEFENSE
A self-contained neon tower-defense built in a single HTML file — no engine, no dependencies. Hold a hero card to inspect it, drag it onto the grid to build, and hold the line across twelve campaign missions and endless quick-play runs.
